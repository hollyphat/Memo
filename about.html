Mobile Memorandum

The present method of sending and receiving memorandum at Computer Science department, Federal Polytechnic Ede is completely manual which is characterized with a lot of problems such as time wastage, lost of memorandum, data insecurity and wrong delivery of memorandum

Aim and Objectives

The major aim of the research work is to develop a system that facilitate the sending and receiving of office memorandum electronically using mobile technology so as to create a "Paperless Office" and allow groups of workers to share documents and files electronically. The objectives of the system include:

  • To design a system that enhances quick and timely delivery of office memorandum.
  • To design a system that facilitate reliable and accurate decision making through instant memorandum delivery on users mobile phones
  • To develop a system that facilitates staff application for facilities and services online.
